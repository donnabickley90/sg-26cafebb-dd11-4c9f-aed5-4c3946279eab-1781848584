## Vision
A private, local-first digital planner called "Kitten's 2026 Chaos Planner" for a single user. It acts as a command centre combining hourly scheduling, meal planning, Chaos Cleaner integration (room-based chore tracking), a declutter challenge, and birthday reminders into one unified interface. Zero cloud services, 100% local browser storage.

## Design
**Style:** Dark soft-goth / cyber-kawaii aesthetic. Organised chaos, rounded cards, subtle pink/purple glows, progress rings, and themed icons (moon, stars, paw prints, sparkles). Dark-mode-first.

**Tokens:**
- `--background`: 260 20% 8% (very dark charcoal/black)
- `--foreground`: 260 10% 90% (silver/soft white)
- `--primary`: 330 100% 70% (hot pink)
- `--secondary`: 270 50% 25% (deep purple)
- `--accent`: 270 70% 80% (soft lilac)
- `--muted`: 260 20% 15% (dark charcoal)
- `--card`: 260 25% 12% (elevated charcoal)
- `--border`: 270 50% 25% (deep purple borders)

**Typography:** Headings: `Quicksand` or `Fredoka`. Body: `Nunito`.

## Features
- **Total Local Privacy:** No accounts, no login. Optional JSON export/import.
- **Dashboard & Stats:** Comprehensive daily hub with quick actions, progress rings, and streaks.
- **2026 Daily Planner:** 5 AM - 11 PM hourly blocks, mood/spoons tracking, brain dump.
- **Meals & Dates:** Reusable weekly meal plans. Birthdays with gift tracking.
- **Chaos Cleaner Engine:** Room-based cleaning cards (11 predefined rooms), Daily/Weekly resets, Deep Clean Calendar, and a 30-Day Declutter Challenge.
